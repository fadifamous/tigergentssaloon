Policy

Privacy, in plain language.

This website is designed to collect as little personal information as possible.

Effective 19 July 2026

What this website does

This website presents information about Tiger Gents Salon and provides links to Fresha for booking and Google Maps for directions. It does not collect names, phone numbers, appointment details, or payment information.

Booking data

When you follow a booking link, you leave this website and continue to Fresha. Fresha handles the information required to create and manage your appointment under its own terms and privacy notices.

Technical information

The hosting provider may process standard request information such as IP address, device details, requested pages, and security logs to operate and protect the website. Retention is determined by the selected hosting provider.

Site preferences

We use local storage to remember whether you accepted optional analytics or chose essential-only use. This preference stays in your browser and can be cleared through browser settings.

Analytics

No analytics provider is enabled in this source build. The website contains privacy-conscious event hooks for future deployment, but they do not transmit information unless an approved analytics platform is configured after consent review.

External websites

Fresha and Google Maps are external services. Their privacy practices apply after you open their websites.

Your choices

You may use the site without accepting optional analytics. You can clear local preferences at any time in your browser. For booking-data requests, use the controls provided by Fresha.

Updates

This policy will be updated if the website begins collecting contact information, enables analytics, or adds new integrations.
