Accessibility

Designed to be usable.

We aim for a clear, keyboard-friendly, responsive experience aligned with WCAG 2.2 Level AA.

Reviewed 19 July 2026

Supported features

  • Skip link and semantic landmarks
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation, filters, FAQ items, and image dialog
  • Visible focus indicators
  • Text alternatives for meaningful images
  • Responsive reflow to 320 pixels
  • Reduced-motion support
  • Touch targets designed around a 44–48 pixel minimum
  • No essential information available only on hover

External booking

Booking is completed through Setmore. Accessibility of that booking flow is controlled by Setmore once you leave this website.

Known limitations

Our primary logo is a detailed raster mark and may lose fine decorative detail at very small sizes; its text alternative provides the salon name. Team profiles currently focus on names and roles.
