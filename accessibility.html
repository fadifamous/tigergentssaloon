Accessibility

Designed to be usable.

We aim for a clear, keyboard-friendly, responsive experience aligned with WCAG 2.2 Level AA.

Reviewed 19 July 2026

Supported features

  • Skip link and semantic landmarks
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation, filters, FAQ items, and image dialog
  • Visible focus indicators
  • Text alternatives for meaningful images
  • Responsive reflow to 320 pixels
  • Reduced-motion support
  • Touch targets designed around a 44–48 pixel minimum
  • No essential information available only on hover

External booking

Booking is completed on Fresha. Accessibility of the booking flow is controlled by Fresha once you leave this website.

Known limitations

The supplied primary logo is a detailed raster mark and may lose fine decorative detail at very small sizes; its text alternative provides the salon name. Team portraits and individual biography details are omitted because verified source material was not supplied.

Feedback

A public phone number, email address, or contact form has not been supplied for this build. Until a verified contact method is approved, use the Fresha profile for booking-related support.
