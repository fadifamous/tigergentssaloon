Cookie choices.

The source build uses one local preference and no advertising cookies.

Effective 19 July 2026

Essential preference storage

The site stores tiger-cookie-choice in your browser’s local storage after you select “Accept optional” or “Essential only.” This prevents the preference notice from showing on every page.

Optional analytics

No analytics vendor is enabled in this build. If one is configured later, it should load only in accordance with the selected consent approach and applicable requirements.

External services

Fresha and Google Maps may use their own cookies after you follow an external link. Those cookies are controlled by the respective service, not this website.

Changing your choice

Clear the website’s stored data in your browser to reset the preference. The notice will appear again on your next visit.
