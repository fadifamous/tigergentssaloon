How do I book an appointment?

Use any “Book appointment” link on this website. Our online booking page opens in a new tab so you can choose a service, professional, and time.

Where is Tiger Gents Salon?

Lake Central Tower, Marasi Drive, Business Bay, Dubai. Use the directions link to open the approved Google Maps location.

What are the opening hours?

We are open from 10:00 AM–12:00 AM, Monday through Sunday. Our online booking page shows current appointment availability.

Can I choose a team member?

Yes. The current team is ABED, Shahem, Joe, and Tiya. Choose your preferred professional while booking, subject to current availability.

Where can I see prices and durations?

Our Services page presents a featured selection. Continue to online booking to browse our complete catalogue, pricing, durations, and current availability.

What services are available?

Our service categories include hair, shaving, hair treatment, manicure, pedicure and foot spa, facial, massage, express nail services, and extras.

Do I need to create an account here?

No. This website does not create client accounts. The secure booking service handles the details required for your appointment.

Can I contact the salon directly?

Yes. Call +971 56 228 5900 or use WhatsApp from the desktop header, mobile action bar, or Contact page.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

Arrive in time for the appointment and be ready to explain the result you want. Your professional can confirm the intended finish before beginning.
